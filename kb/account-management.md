# Account Management

## Security Settings

Manage your account security from **Settings** > **Security**:

- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable via authenticator app or SMS.
- **Reset Password:** Change your password (see password-reset article).
- **Active Sessions:** View and revoke logged-in devices.
- **API Keys:** Generate and rotate API keys for integrations.

We recommend enabling 2FA on all accounts.

## Updating Your Profile

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Profile Settings**.
3. Update your display name, email, or timezone.
4. Click **Save Changes**.

Email changes require verification of the new address.

## Deleting Your Account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Danger Zone**.
2. Click **Delete Account**.
3. Type your password to confirm.
4. Click **Permanently Delete**.

All data will be removed within 30 days per our data retention policy.

## Transferring Account Ownership

Organization owners can transfer ownership:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Organization** > **Members**.
2. Click the member you want to promote.
3. Select **Transfer Ownership**.
4. Confirm via email verification.

Only one owner is allowed per organization.

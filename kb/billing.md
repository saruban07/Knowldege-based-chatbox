# Billing & Subscriptions

## Viewing Your Invoice

To view or download invoices:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**.
2. Click **Invoice History**.
3. Select the invoice you want to view.
4. Click **Download PDF** to save a copy.

Invoices are available for the past 24 months.

## Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing** > **Payment Methods**.
2. Click **Add Payment Method**.
3. Enter your card or bank details.
4. Set the new method as default if desired.
5. Click **Save**.

Your next charge will use the default payment method on file.

## Subscription Plans

We offer three plans:

| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0/mo | Basic features, 1 user |
| Pro | $29/mo | Advanced features, 5 users |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited users, SLA, dedicated support |

To upgrade or downgrade:

1. Open **Settings** > **Billing** > **Subscription**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select your desired plan and confirm.

Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle unless you choose immediate upgrade.

## Cancellation Policy

You may cancel anytime. Access continues until the end of the current billing period. No refunds for partial months unless required by law.

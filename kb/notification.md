# Notifications

## Overview

Notifications help you stay informed about important account activity, billing updates, security events, team collaboration, and product announcements.

You can customize notification preferences to receive only the information that matters to you.

---

## Managing Notification Settings

To manage notifications:

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Navigate to **Notifications**.
3. Review available notification categories.
4. Enable or disable the desired options.
5. Click **Save Changes**.

Changes take effect immediately.

---

## Email Notifications

Email notifications are sent to your registered email address.

Available email notification types include:

* Product Updates
* Billing Alerts
* Security Alerts
* Account Activity
* Team Invitations
* Ticket Updates
* Scheduled Reports
* Integration Notifications

Email notifications help you stay informed even when you are not logged into the platform.

---

## Billing Alerts

Billing notifications are automatically sent when:

* An invoice is generated
* A payment succeeds
* A payment fails
* A subscription is upgraded
* A subscription is downgraded
* A subscription is about to expire

These alerts help prevent service interruptions.

---

## Security Alerts

Security notifications are triggered by important account events.

Examples include:

* Password changes
* Password reset requests
* New device logins
* Failed login attempts
* Two-factor authentication changes
* API key creation or deletion

We strongly recommend keeping security alerts enabled.

---

## Product Updates

Receive information about:

* New features
* Platform improvements
* Maintenance announcements
* Feature deprecations
* Upcoming releases

Product updates help users stay informed about platform changes.

---

## Ticket Notifications

Users receive notifications when:

* A support ticket is created
* A ticket is assigned
* A ticket status changes
* A support agent replies
* A ticket is resolved

Ticket notifications can be delivered through email and in-app alerts.

---

## Push Notifications

Push notifications are available on supported mobile applications.

To enable push notifications:

1. Open the mobile application.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Notifications**.
3. Enable Push Notifications.
4. Allow notification permissions on your device.

Push notifications provide real-time updates even when the app is closed.

---

## In-App Notifications

In-app notifications appear within the platform while you are logged in.

Examples include:

* New comments
* Team mentions
* System announcements
* Integration updates
* Security warnings

Unread notifications are displayed in the notification center.

---

## Notification Frequency

Users can control how frequently notifications are delivered.

Available options:

* Instant
* Hourly Digest
* Daily Digest
* Weekly Summary

Digest notifications combine multiple updates into a single message.

---

## Managing Notification Preferences

You can customize notifications by category.

Examples:

* Receive billing alerts only
* Disable product updates
* Enable security alerts
* Receive team activity notifications

Each notification type can be configured independently.

---

## Unsubscribing from Emails

To stop receiving non-essential emails:

1. Open any notification email.
2. Click the **Unsubscribe** link.
3. Confirm your preference.

Important security and billing notifications may still be sent even if marketing emails are disabled.

---

## Troubleshooting Notifications

### I Am Not Receiving Emails

Check the following:

* Spam or junk folder
* Email address accuracy
* Notification settings
* Email server restrictions

Wait up to 15 minutes for delivery during peak periods.

### Push Notifications Are Not Working

Verify:

* Notification permissions are enabled
* The mobile app is updated
* Device battery optimization is not blocking notifications
* Internet connectivity is available

### Receiving Too Many Notifications

Adjust notification frequency or disable unnecessary categories under **Settings > Notifications**.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I enable email notifications?

Go to **Settings > Notifications** and enable the desired email notification categories.

### How do I turn off notifications?

Open **Settings > Notifications**, disable the categories you no longer want, and save your changes.

### Why am I not receiving security alerts?

Check your notification preferences, spam folder, and registered email address.

### Can I receive notifications on my phone?

Yes. Push notifications are supported through the mobile application.

### Can I receive daily summaries instead of individual alerts?

Yes. Select **Daily Digest** under notification frequency settings.

### How do I unsubscribe from emails?

Click the unsubscribe link included in any notification email and confirm your preference.

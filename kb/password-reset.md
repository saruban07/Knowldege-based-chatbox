# Password Reset Guide

## Resetting Passwords

If you forgot your password or need to change it for security reasons, follow these steps:

1. Open **Settings** from the main dashboard menu.
2. Click **Security** in the left sidebar.
3. Select **Reset Password**.
4. Enter your current password (if prompted).
5. Choose a new password that meets these requirements:
   - At least 12 characters
   - One uppercase letter
   - One lowercase letter
   - One number
   - One special character
6. Click **Save** to confirm the change.

You will receive a confirmation email once your password has been updated.

## Forgot Your Password?

If you cannot remember your current password:

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password?**
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 24 hours).
5. Click the link and set a new password.

## Password Reset Not Working?

If the reset email does not arrive:

- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Verify you entered the correct email address.
- Wait up to 10 minutes for delivery.
- Contact support if the issue persists after 30 minutes.
